    Skilled Nursing

    Skilled nursing provides professional medical care at home, including wound care, medication management, injections, and monitoring of health conditions. It ensures patients receive expert care from licensed nurses without needing hospital visits.

    • Medication management
    • Wound care
    • Chronic disease management
    • Post-hospitalization recovery

    Therapy Services

    Therapy services include physical, occupational, and speech therapy to help individuals regain strength, mobility, and communication skills. These services support recovery from injuries, surgeries, or medical conditions, improving overall quality of life.

    • Physical therapy
    • Occupational therapy
    • Speech therapy

    Personal Care Assistance

    Personal care assistance helps individuals with daily activities such as bathing, dressing, grooming, and mobility support. It promotes independence while ensuring safety and comfort at home.

    • Bathing & grooming
    • Mobility support
    • Chronic disease management
    • Meal preparation

    Companionship Services

    Companionship services provide social engagement and emotional support, reducing feelings of loneliness and isolation. Caregivers offer friendly conversation, assistance with errands, and help with light household tasks.

    • Bathing & grooming
    • Mobility support
    • Chronic disease management
    • Meal preparation
