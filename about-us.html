    Care & Amenities

    At Home Not Alone Home Health, we are dedicated to providing compassionate, high-quality care that allows individuals to live safely and independently in the comfort of their own homes. Founded on the principles of dignity, respect, and excellence, we strive to make a positive impact in the lives of our clients and their families every day.

    Contact us

    Our team of skilled professionals, including nurses, therapists, and caregivers, works collaboratively to deliver personalized care plans tailored to each client's unique needs. From skilled nursing and therapy services to personal care and companionship, we are committed to enhancing the quality of life for those we serve.

    Services

    Here are a few of our services

    Skilled Nursing

    Medical care at home, including wound care, medications, and health monitoring.

    Therapy Services

    Physical, occupational, and speech therapy for recovery and mobility.

    Personal Care Assistance

    Help with bathing, dressing, and daily activities for independence.
